Room Allocation

Efficient digital management of hostel rooms

Overview

The Room Allocation module enables admins to assign and manage hostel rooms based on availability, capacity, and student preferences. It automates record-keeping and ensures optimal utilization of space.

Key Features

  • Add, edit, and delete hostel rooms with block/floor details.
  • Allocate rooms to students automatically or manually.
  • Real-time status: occupied, available, or maintenance.
  • Generate occupancy reports for admin review.

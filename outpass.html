Outpass Management

Digital approval and verification of student outings

Overview

The Outpass Management module allows students to apply for day or home outings digitally. Wardens review and approve requests, while security verifies at the hostel gate. This ensures transparency, eliminates manual paper slips, and enhances safety.

Key Features

  • Apply for Day or Home outings with reason and time.
  • Warden approval with remarks and digital signatures.
  • Security gate check-out and check-in verification.
  • Real-time status tracking and automated logs.

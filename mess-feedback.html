Mess Feedback

Collecting student feedback to improve food quality

Overview

The Mess Feedback module gathers daily or weekly feedback from students regarding food quality, taste, and hygiene. It enables the administration to analyze trends and continuously improve services.

Key Features

  • Star-rating system and text feedback.
  • Admin dashboard showing average ratings and trends.
  • Anonymous feedback option for honest responses.
  • Automated reports to mess management.

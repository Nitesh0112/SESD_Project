Key Modules

Outpass Management

Students request outings; wardens approve them; security verifies at the gate.

Room Allocation

Admins assign rooms efficiently based on availability and capacity with live tracking.

Complaint Tracking

Students raise maintenance complaints and track resolution progress through the dashboard.

Visitor Management

Security digitally records visitor details, ensuring transparent and secure access.

Mess Feedback

Students submit food ratings and comments; admins analyze satisfaction trends.

Notice Board

Admins publish digital notices instantly viewable to all hostel residents.
